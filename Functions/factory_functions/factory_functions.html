Color Theory

  1. RGB (RED GREEN BLUE) - rgb(22,123,22)
  2. RGBA (RED GREEN BLUE ALPHA) - rgba(1,22,123,0.7)

    3. In rgba we have Red color number, then Green number, then Blue number, then Alpha channel for Opacity. ranging from 0.0 to 1.0.

  3. HEX (HEXADECIMAL VALUE) - #e67ff34
  4. HSL (HUE SATURATION LIGHTNESS)

    5. We have a number from 0 - 360 for hue . Then a percentage for saturation. Then a percentage for lightness.
